About Spread a Smile

Transforming education through student-led initiatives

Our Mission

Spread a Smile (SAS) is a community service initiative where B.Tech college students visit nearby government schools to interact with children and teach innovative concepts using their unique styles and perspectives.

How We Operate

1. Volunteer Registration

B.Tech students from all years and departments register as volunteers.

2. Team Formation

Volunteers are divided into balanced teams based on year and department.

3. School Coordination

Teams are assigned to different classes in partner government schools.

4. Regular Visits

Scheduled visits where students conduct interactive learning sessions.

5. Documentation

Each visit is documented with reports, photos, and videos.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable, long-term community activity that bridges the gap between college students and underprivileged children, fostering mutual learning and growth while bringing smiles through education.
