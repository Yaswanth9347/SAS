About Spread a Smile

Transforming education through student-led initiatives

Our Mission

Spread a Smile (SAS) is a community service initiative where B.Tech college students visit nearby government schools to interact with children and teach innovative concepts using their unique styles and perspectives. We believe every child deserves access to quality education and mentorship, regardless of their socioeconomic background.

Through our visits, we aim to inspire young minds, introduce them to new ideas, and create lasting connections between college students and the communities they serve.

How We Operate

📝

1. Volunteer Registration

B.Tech students from all years and departments register as volunteers through our platform.

👥

2. Team Formation

Volunteers are divided into balanced teams based on year, department, and availability.

🏫

3. School Coordination

Teams are assigned to different classes in partner government schools in the region.

📚

4. Regular Visits

Scheduled visits where students conduct interactive learning sessions and activities.

📸

5. Documentation

Each visit is documented with detailed reports, photos, and videos for tracking impact.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable, long-term community activity that bridges the gap between college students and underprivileged children, fostering mutual learning and growth while bringing smiles through education.

We envision a future where every student becomes a change-maker in their community, and every child has access to inspiring mentors who can help them realize their full potential.
